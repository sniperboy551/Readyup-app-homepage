About ReadyUp

ReadyUp is a simple, private scheduling app designed for groups of friends or teammates who can never seem to find a day when everyone’s free.

Each week, users fill out their availability on a shared weekly timetable. Everyone’s schedule resets every Monday — keeping things fresh, simple, and focused on the week ahead.

Whether you’re planning gaming sessions, hangouts, or study groups, ReadyUp makes it easy to spot overlap and lock in the best day for everyone.

Core Features

Weekly Reset

Schedules automatically reset every week, so your plans always stay current.

Shared Timetable

Everyone in your group can see when others are free — no messages or guesswork.

Private Circles

Only invited friends can view or edit the shared schedule.

Simple Login

Sign in securely with your Google account to save and sync your availability.
